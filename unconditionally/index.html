introduction

So i've recently become a rather big fan of machineheart's work, and i was going through some of their old videos and comparing them (all covers) to the originals, and one pairing in particular caught my eye: Unconditionally, originally by katy perry. The contrast between the two approaches was huge, and i couldn't help but take some time to analyse how that made me feel. To put it lightly, katy perry's video was not my fav.

But it's not enough for me to just go I didn't like that video meh meh mah; i want to know why. What was it about machineheart's act that made me appreciate it so much more? That is what we are here to find out.
